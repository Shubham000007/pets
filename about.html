Passionate Family Owned Pet Boarding Business in Onkaparinga Hills

At Dogs and Mogs, we are a family-owned and operated business. We offer affordable boarding kennels and catteries in the convenient location of Onkaparinga Hills, South Australia. If your need someone to look after your 4-legged family member while you're away from home, you can trust the friendly and passionate staff at Dogs and Mogs to take the best care of your pets.

Affordable Daily Rates

At Dogs and Mogs, we cater to all cats and dogs of any size. We offer a range of daily rates that vary depending on the size of your Pet.

*Our daily rates are:

Cats: $13.00

Small Dogs: $23.00

Medium Dogs: $24.00

Large Dogs: $26.00

Extra Large Dogs: $27.00

Minimum 2 days charge applicable.

Easter and Christmas periods 5 days minimum charge required.

*Please note that prices are per day, prices may change without prior notice and prices may vary during public holidays
